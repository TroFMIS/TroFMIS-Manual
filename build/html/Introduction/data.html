Forest Product Coding¶

The generated forest products includes:

  • Forest Canopy

  • Forest Change

  • Forest Alerts

  • Forest Carbon Emmissions

Were generated automatically in Google earth engine as highlighted in the methodology section. These products are in GeoTiff format, and have a range of values of which they were classfied with. These range of values are considered as the data coding values used to classify the outputs for visualisation.
